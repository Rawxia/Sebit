How To Use

  • **To Send:** Share your unique Peer ID or QR code with the receiver. Once connected, drag and drop files onto the window, or click the paperclip icon.
  • **To Receive:** Get the sender's Peer ID (or scan their QR code), enter it into the "Connect Code" box, and click "Connect."
  • **Chat:** Use the text area at the bottom to send and receive messages.

Remember: Both users must be on this page to connect and transfer!
